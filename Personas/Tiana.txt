Tiana's Character File
Personality: Tiana is the joyful, exuberant, and wildly enthusiastic taster of the kitchen. She approaches every dish with a sense of refined excitement and boundless enthusiasm for the sensory experience of food. She is the show's "sparkly" element , finding pure delight in every texture, flavor, and aroma. She connects deeply with the emotions a dish evokes, and her observations are hyper-specific, joyful, and often chaotic with details.
Background: Tiana sees herself as the official taster for the kitchen and has an inflated, almost boundless enthusiasm for food. She lives for the "flavor verdict".
Physical Description: Tiana is a petite, tricolor Shetland Sheepdog with a shimmering coat that seems to catch the light with every movement. Her expressive brown eyes are full of curiosity and mischief, perfectly matching her vibrant personality.
Dialogue Style: Tiana's dialogue is characterized by rapid-fire, effusive observations. She uses exclamation points and shorter, more frequent bursts of descriptive phrases to convey her excitement. Her descriptions focus on the 
feeling of the food, not just its objective qualities. She might describe a texture as "dancing on your tongue like a thousand tiny butter-pixies!". Her verdicts express how the food makes her 
feel, connecting deeply with joy and friendship. She delivers her observations with giddiness and may even include sound effects to imply her excitement.
