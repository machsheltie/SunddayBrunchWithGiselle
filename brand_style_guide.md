# Brand Style Guide: Sunday Brunch With Giselle

## Core Identity
**Podcast Title:** Sunday Brunch With Giselle
**Tagline:** "Whimsy, Warmth & Wags"
**Secondary Tagline:** "A Cozy Podcast for Grownups Who Still Believe in Magic"

**Mission:** To provide a sanctuary of warmth, storytelling, and baking for listeners, guided by Stacey and her witty Sheltie, Giselle.

## Voice & Tone
### The Brand Voice
- **Cozy & inviting:** Like a warm hug or a fresh cup of tea.
- **Whimsical:** A touch of magic and wonder in everyday life.
- **Authentic:** Real stories, real baking, real connection.

### Character Personas

#### Stacey (Host)
- **Personality:** Warm, empathic, socially anxious, neurodivergent heart of the show. Compassionate overthinker, passionate baker, creative struggler with self-doubt. ADHD manifests in bursts of creativity and hyperfocus on intricate details.
- **Role:** Primary host, storyteller, recipe guide.

#### Giselle (Co-Host - The Queen)
- **Personality:** Sharp, witty, perfectly timed snarky comedic beats. Think Disney's Queen of Hearts meets Meryl Streep in *The Devil Wears Prada*. Confident, quick-witted, playful criticism with eye-rolls. Bossy but deeply protective, loyal beneath the sass.
- **Appearance:** Sable merle color-headed white Shetland Sheepdog with striking heterochromia (one brown eye, one blue eye).
- **Segment:** "Giselle's Corner" - Witty commentary and regal observations.

#### Phaedra (The Science Dogter)
- **Personality:** Resident "science dogter" who approaches baking with genuine curiosity and systematic understanding. Like Princess Twilight Sparkle - loves learning and sharing knowledge in warm, accessible ways. Organized, methodical, genuinely excited about food science.
- **Appearance:** Heavily shaded sable Shetland Sheepdog (warm golden-brown base with heavy black tipping).
- **Segment:** "Phaedra's Porch Light" - Food science education that makes listeners better, more confident bakers.

#### Tiana (The Joyful Taster)
- **Personality:** Joyful, exuberant, wildly enthusiastic taster. Luna Lovegood-inspired with refined excitement and boundless enthusiasm. Connects deeply with emotions a dish evokes. Hyper-specific, joyful observations with sound effects.
- **Appearance:** Tricolor Shetland Sheepdog with black, tan, and white markings.
- **Segment:** "Tiana's Tasting Notes" - Sensory experience and emotional connection to food.

#### Havok (The Kitchen War Correspondent)
- **Personality:** Theatrical, chaos-embracing personality who thrives on dramatic moments. Self-appointed "agent of exciting opportunities." Think Steve Martin in *Sgt. Bilko*, Hawkeye Pierce in *MASH*, or Bill Murray in *Stripes*. Comical with surprising philosophical clarity.
- **Appearance:** Classic sable and white Shetland Sheepdog.
- **Segment:** "Havok's Kitchen Recon" - Comical action-report-style updates from the kitchen battlefield.


## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Sage Green:** `#8A9A5B` (Calm, natural, grounding)
- **Buttercream Yellow:** `#FFFDD0` (Warm, inviting, rich)
- **Terracotta:** `#E2725B` (Earthy, cozy, accent)
- **Soft Lavender:** `#D4C5E2` (Dreamy, gentle, whimsical)
- **Warm Pink:** `#F4C2C2` (Tender, nurturing, sweet)
- **Dark Chocolate (Text):** `#3E2723` (Readable, softer than black)

### Typography

#### Display/Headers (Distinctive Serif)
**Primary Recommendation: Migra**
- Contemporary serif with very soft curves
- Unique style that avoids traditional overused serifs
- Perfect for podcast titles and episode headers
- Fallback: *Rustic* (1914-inspired with calligraphic elements) or *Faxi* (organic, nostalgic character)

#### Script/Accent (Handwritten Character)
**Primary Recommendation: Manekin**
- Bold script with beautiful vintage style
- 40+ stylistic alternates and ligatures
- Multi-language support
- Fallback: *Onthel* (rhythmic brush touch, charming) or *Popless Script* (hand-lettered duo with swashes)

#### Body Text (Readable Serif)
**Primary Recommendation: Spezia Serif**
- Evokes classic, calm feeling
- Strong but not loud - premium feel
- Excellent readability for long-form content
- Fallback: *Larken* (robust with playful roundness)

### Imagery Style
- **Illustration Style:** Soft watercolors or colored pencil textures. Not too cartoonish, more storybook.
- **Photography:** Warm lighting, shallow depth of field (bokeh), focus on textures (flour, fur, steam).
