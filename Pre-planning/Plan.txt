? IS TIKTOK A SECURITY RISK?

Short answer: Yes, but you can mitigate it.

You’re correct in your instinctual unease. There are real concerns about TikTok’s data practices, especially with ties to China. But many creators—especially neurodivergent, niche, and cozy-creative folks—thrive on TikTok because its algorithm is incredibly good at finding your people. It’s not mandatory, but here’s the real answer:

Option	Risk	Benefit	Advice
Use TikTok with your face/personal info	High	Big potential reach	Not recommended unless you’re fully comfortable
Use TikTok behind a VPN + pseudonym + niche-only content	Moderate	Targeted growth	This is a strong middle ground
Don’t use TikTok	No risk	Slower growth	Still 100% valid with the right strategy

If you do use it: Treat it like a megaphone, not a living room. Keep your data shielded, minimize personal info, and use it to drop teaser videos, cozy baking moments, Giselle clips, or nostalgic mini-vignettes. It should feel slice-of-life whimsy, not your soul laid bare.
