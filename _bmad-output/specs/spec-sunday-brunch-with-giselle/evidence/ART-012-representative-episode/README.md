## Evidence Contract

ART-012 is accepted only when one real representative episode traces an approved brief through production, release, and archive.

The package must identify:

- Stable episode ID, version, owner, lifecycle state, and canonical source path
- Approved episode brief, final script, canon version, cast, and character purpose by scene
- Factual, recipe, safety, grief, consent, rights, and human-voice-only review
- Source recordings and generated assets stored separately
- Traceability from script through generated or recorded dialogue, edits, and release master
- Voice-asset records, music and effects rights, and human listening review
- Stacey's approval of every final character line
- Corrected speaker-labeled transcript, chapters, artwork, credits, show notes, and accessibility review
- AI-performance disclosure and its placement
- Featured recipe or companion article and one clear audience action
- Publication metadata, podcast-host record, release identifiers, scheduling or publication evidence, and fallback
- Archive and backup references for source, master, transcript, artwork, metadata, and rights records
- Correction, rollback, withdrawal, and restoration ownership and procedures
- Accepted ART-007 episode validation evidence
- Stacey's final approval
