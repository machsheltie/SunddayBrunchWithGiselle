# Audio Production Template Guide

## Episode Structure (Based on Episode 1)

### Total Episode Length: 30-35 minutes

### Segment Breakdown

#### 1. **Intro** (2-3 minutes)
- Theme music
- Welcome/greeting
- Episode title and recipe announcement
- Sensory kitchen sounds (clinking, pouring)
- Set the cozy atmosphere

#### 2. **Before We Bake** (3-4 minutes)
- Ritual segment - creating the sacred space
- Lighting candles, preparing workspace
- Giselle's witty commentary
- Transition to story

#### 3. **Recipe Story** (5-8 minutes)
- Personal narrative about the recipe
- Emotional connection, memories
- Why this recipe matters
- Build anticipation

#### 4. **Sheltie Segments** (6-8 minutes total)
Each segment: 90 seconds to 2 minutes
- **Giselle's Corner:** Witty observations about the recipe
- **Phaedra's Porch Light:** Food science explanation
- **Tiana's Tasting Notes:** Sensory/emotional experience
- **Havok's Kitchen Recon:** Comical action report

#### 5. **Recipe Walkthrough** (9-10 minutes)
- "Let's Bake It Together" segment
- Step-by-step instructions
- Tips and techniques
- Encouragement and troubleshooting

#### 6. **Next Week Preview** (1-2 minutes)
- Tease next episode's recipe
- Build excitement

#### 7. **Outro** (2-3 minutes)
- Wrap-up and reflection
- Call to action (social media, website)
- Theme music fade out

## Sound Effects Library

### Essential Kitchen Sounds
- **Mixing:** Whisk in bowl, electric mixer
- **Pouring:** Liquid pouring, dry ingredients
- **Chopping:** Knife on cutting board
- **Oven:** Door opening/closing, timer beep
- **Utensils:** Spoon stirring, spatula scraping
- **Containers:** Jar opening, lid closing

### Atmospheric Sounds
- **Cozy Ambience:** Soft kitchen background, gentle hum
- **Candle Lighting:** Match strike, soft flame
- **Tea/Coffee:** Kettle whistle, cup placement
- **Paper:** Recipe card rustling

### Transition Sounds
- **Segment Transitions:** Gentle chime, soft whoosh
- **Character Entrances:** Subtle "paw step" or character-specific cue
  - Giselle: Regal chime
  - Phaedra: Book page turn
  - Tiana: Sparkle/twinkle
  - Havok: Comedic "action" sting

### Emotional Underscoring
- **Story Segment:** Warm, nostalgic piano or strings
- **Recipe Walkthrough:** Upbeat, encouraging background
- **Sheltie Segments:** Character-specific musical themes

## Mixing & Mastering Guidelines

### Target Audio Levels
- **Dialogue:** -6dB to -3dB peak
- **Music:** -20dB to -15dB
- **SFX:** -15dB to -10dB
- **Ambience:** -25dB to -20dB

### Effects Chain for Voice Tracks
1. **Noise Reduction** (if needed)
2. **Compressor**
   - Threshold: -12dB
   - Ratio: 3:1
   - Attack: 0.2s
   - Release: 1.0s
3. **Equalization**
   - Boost: 100-200Hz (warmth)
   - Cut: 3-5kHz (harshness)
   - Boost: 8-12kHz (clarity)

### Final Master
1. **Limiter**
   - Type: Hard Limit
   - Limit to: -1.0dB
2. **Normalize**
   - Peak amplitude: -1.0dB
