# ART-002 Product Direction Approval

- Status: accepted
- Approver: Stacey
- Approval date: 2026-06-11
- Decision: D-01

## Approved Market Position

> The most comforting, character-rich baking destination that is also one of the easiest recipe sites to cook from.

## Approved Launch Promise

> Sunday Brunch With Giselle helps pet-loving home bakers make something comforting and dependable while sharing a warm, whimsical Sunday with Stacey, Giselle, and the Sheltie family.

## Approved Audience Jobs

1. Choose something worth baking.
2. Understand whether it can be made successfully.
3. Complete the recipe without confusion.
4. Have company while baking.
5. Recover when something goes wrong.
6. Return for a dependable Sunday ritual.
7. Participate in a warm pet-loving community only when that community is ready.

## Approved Content Pillars

1. The Sunday Table
2. Recipes From Giselle's Kitchen
3. Tales From the Kitchen
4. The Sunday Brunch Podcast
5. Friends At Our Table
6. The Gentle Kitchen

## Approved Production Streams

1. Cook With Stacey and Giselle
2. Sunday Brunch Podcast
3. Notes From the Shelties
4. Stories From Our Table
5. Friends At Our Table

Friends At Our Table remains future-only until community readiness gates pass.

## Taxonomy Mapping Evidence

| French Silk Pie recipe | Recipes From Giselle's Kitchen | Cook With Stacey and Giselle | Complete a dependable bake without confusion | Stacey |

## Rationale

The approved direction binds emotional distinction to dependable baking utility. The pillars organize audience discovery; the streams organize production responsibility.
