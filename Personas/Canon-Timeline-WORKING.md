1. **Timeline:** Does any dog appear in a scene before they were born or after they passed? (Use the table above. Giselle ⇒ not before ~2014. Athena ⇒ 2004–2021.)
2. **Eternal-present rule:** Giselle may *interject/comment* as the eternal queen on any story, but should not be placed *bodily* in a scene she couldn't have witnessed. Put the real dog of that era in-scene (e.g., Athena for 2009).
3. **Human-voice-only:** Are real memories, grief, safety, allergens, and corrections all in Stacey's voice (never a dog narrating a real memory)?
4. **Kinship accuracy:** Any stated relationship matches the family tree in the pack bible? (Phaedra = Havok's aunt; Giselle = Tiana's mom; etc.)
5. **Facts a dog states are true** (Havok's subs, Phaedra's science) — or flagged as "unconfirmed intel."
